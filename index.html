About Me

Hello! My name is Kyle San. I am a game designer and programmer. Since a young age I have always loved creating and playing games. I started making pen and paper games in elementary school and am now studying at USC to achieve my dream of becoming a game developer.

I believe that having a technical understanding of games contributes greatly to being a good game developer and therefore work to keep my programming skills sharp. I started programming in highschool by taking online courses in Python. For my senior project, I learned Java and created a simple 2d game from scratch. At USC I picked up Unity and C# and participated in numerous game jams and hackathons while master C++ and Java in class.

In my spare time I like to work on personal projects and learn new game engines. I also enjoy collaborating with friends in game jams and hackathons. Outside of gaming my hobbies include astronomy, hiking, and swimming. I am fascinated by space and the beauty of nature and swim regularly to maintain an active lifestyle.

I am currently looking for internship opportunities for summer 2015.
